# Halfling Shadow Monk (Warrior of Shadow) 19 / Rogue 1 (2024 PHB)

### Core Build

* **Race:** Halfling
* **Classes:** Rogue 1 → Warrior of Shadow Monk 19
* **Origin Feat:** Tavern Brawler
* **Stats (final):** 21 Dex / 20 Wis / 14 Con / 10 Int / 8 Str / 8 Cha
* **ASIs:** +2 Dex, +2 Wis, +2 Dex, +2 Wis
* **Epic Boon:** Night Spirit (+1 Dex)
* **Expertise:** Acrobatics, Stealth
* **Weapon Masteries:**

  * Dagger (Nick)
  * Shortbow (Vex)

## Game Plan

Use **Nick** with daggers to add an extra attack, then convert your Bonus Action into **Flurry of Blows**.

At Monk 10 (character level 11):

* 2 dagger attacks (Extra Attack)
* 1 Nick attack
* 3 Flurry attacks (Heightened Focus)

**Total: 6 attacks each round** for only 1 Focus Point.

Shadow Monk supplies consistent Advantage through its own Darkness, improving accuracy and enabling Sneak Attack.

---

## Why Rogue 1?

You trade Monk's capstone for:

* Nick Weapon Mastery
* Sneak Attack (including opportunity attacks)
* Expertise
* Extra skills
* Shortbow proficiency

Overall this is primarily a utility trade rather than a raw DPR increase.

---

## Why Shadow?

Shadow Monk provides:

* Reliable self-generated Advantage via Darkness.
* Shadow Step mobility.
* Free Flurry while in Dim Light/Darkness at high levels (Cloak of Shadows), conserving Focus.

# Latest Update (May 9, 2026)

The only substantive addition since the original build is support for the **D&D Beyond Drops** feats:

### Fey Pact

* Grants a Wisdom-based ranged cantrip such as:

  * **Mind Sliver**
  * **Vicious Mockery**
* Useful for ranged utility and imposing penalties to enemy saves or attacks.

### Fey Sentinel

Excellent synergy with Monk mobility:

* **Fey Shift** becomes a **Bonus Action teleport** equal to half your speed.
* Combines extremely well with **Step of the Wind**, allowing exceptional battlefield repositioning.
* **Plant Growth** creates difficult terrain that you can freely teleport around, making it a powerful battlefield-control option.

---

Everything else in the guide (variants, Tasha's options, Ranger dip, Rogue 2–3, etc.) remains unchanged. The Fey Pact/Fey Sentinel interaction is the only notable update added after the original build.
